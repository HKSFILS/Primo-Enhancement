E-Platform for Hong Kong Self Financing Institution Libraries



The Open University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong Shue Yan University, Caritas Institute of Higher Education, Chu Hai College of Higher Education, and Tung Wah College collaboratively develop an online collection-sharing and information discovery system to enable faculty members and students to easily find and access library and e-book collections among the five libraries through a one-stop search interface. Through the project, more than 20,000 e-books are acquired. This allows the participating libraries to promote reading while enriching their digital contents.
